A father-and-son partnership combining two generations of expertise to bridge Switzerland and India.

Pullely Consulting is built on a unique foundation: a father and son, two generations of expertise, one shared mission. Together, we combine decades of experience in Swiss finance, technology, and cross-border business development with deep roots in Kerala, India.

Sanoj Pullely

Founding Partner

Born and raised in Switzerland to parents from Kerala, India, Sanoj has lived the connection between these two worlds his entire life. His career began at the University of St. Gallen (HSG), where he co-founded the Asia Club and was recognized for the best research project in "Doing Business in India."

His professional home is in Swiss finance and technology. As a venture capitalist at Zuercher Kantonalbank, he honed his ability to identify and nurture high-growth companies. Combined with roles at Lombard Odier, UBS, Credit Suisse, SIX, and Deloitte, Sanoj brings a unique ability to translate complex opportunities into clear business value for decision-makers.

Our Story

Two generations, one mission

Within Switzerland, the community originating from Kerala represents the most established Indian diaspora in the country. Over decades, this group has built deep cultural and professional roots, fostering trust, resilience, and an openness to collaboration. These values form the foundation of our work today.

Today, this unique combination of cultural insight, investment expertise, and cross-border network is more relevant than ever. With the TEPA Free Trade Agreement between Switzerland and India now in effect, a historic window of opportunity is opening for pioneers and visionaries. We are ready to guide you through this new era of trade and cooperation.
